# RhythmRush

Play rhythm games. Earn rewards. Own your progress.

## What You Get

You play rhythm games. You score points. You earn RUSH tokens. Top players win more. Your scores stay on blockchain. Your rewards stay in your wallet.

## How It Works

Connect your wallet. Buy RUSH tokens with CELO or cUSD. Mint one Gem NFT. Play games. Submit scores. Compete for rewards.

## Your First Game

Step one: Connect your wallet. Use MetaMask, WalletConnect, or MiniPay. MiniPay users get cUSD payment options.

Step two: Buy RUSH tokens. Choose CELO or cUSD. One CELO buys 30 RUSH tokens. Point 17 cUSD buys 30 RUSH tokens. You need 34 RUSH tokens to mint a Gem.

Step three: Mint your Gem. Spend 34 RUSH tokens. One Gem unlocks all games. Your Gem stays in your wallet.

Step four: Play games. Choose RhythmRush or Simon. Tap buttons in sync. Remember patterns. Score points. Games submit scores automatically.

Step five: View leaderboard. See top 10 players. Top three get podium display. Your position shows your rank.

Step six: Claim rewards. Top players earn RUSH tokens. Rewards go to your wallet. Prize pool distributes automatically.

## Games

RhythmRush: Tap glowing buttons in sync. Perfect timing scores 10 points. Good timing scores 5 points. Thirty second rounds. Sound effects guide you.

Simon: Watch button sequences. Repeat patterns correctly. Score based on speed. Wrong answer ends the game.

## Rewards

First place wins 40 percent of prize pool. Second place wins 25 percent. Third place wins 15 percent. Places four through ten split 10 percent. All players split 10 percent participation rewards.

Minimum score is 10 points to qualify.
